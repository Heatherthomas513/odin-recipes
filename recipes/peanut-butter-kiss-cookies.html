Peanut Butter Blossoms



Delicious peanut butter cookies topped with Hershey's Kisses.


Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 4 ounces (1 stick) butter, at room temperature
  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup light brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 5 dozen (one 11-ounce package) Hershey's Kisses with foil removed
  • Nonstick spray or vegetable oil for cookie sheet (optional)

Directions

  1. Sift together flour, baking soda and salt; set aside.
  2. Using an electric mixer, cream together butter, peanut butter, ½ cup granulated sugar and light brown sugar. Add egg, milk and vanilla; beat until well blended. Gradually add flour mixture, mixing thoroughly. If the dough is very soft, refrigerate for about 1 hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  4. Spray, oil or line a cookie sheet with nonstick liner and set aside.
  5. Roll dough into 1-inch balls and place them 2 inches apart on cookie sheet.
  6. Bake until very light brown and puffed, 6 to 8 minutes.
  7. Remove sheet from oven and lightly press a candy kiss into center of each cookie, allowing it to crack slightly.
  8. Return to oven until light golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes.
  9. Remove from oven, cool completely and store in an airtight container.
