Crock Pot Roast with Potatoes and Carrots



Beef roast slow cooked with potatoes, carrots, and onions.


Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 ½ pounds chuck roast
  • 1.1 ounces beefy onion soup mix, dry
  • 1 onion, peeled and quartered
  • 1 ½ tablespoons garlic, peeled and chopped
  • 1 cup celery, washed and cut in chunks
  • 2 ½ pounds baby potatoes, Yukon Gold or Red, washed
  • 1 ½ pounds baby carrots, washed
  • ½ cup water, or beef stock
  • salt & pepper to taste

Directions


  1. Sprinkle the meat with about 1 teaspoon of the onion soup mix and rub it in. Add salt and pepper to taste. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a heavy skillet.
  3. Add onions and celery and saute until the onions start to caramelize.
  4. Add garlic and saute for 1 minute.
  5. Remove vegetables from pan.
  6. Add meat to pan and brown on all sides - about 5 minutes total.
  7. Add the meat, onions and celery to the slow cooker.
  8. Top with remaining ingredients.
  9. Cover, set on low, and set timer for 8 hours.
